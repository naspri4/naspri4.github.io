PAYMENT/INSURANCE

Deposit:

A $500.00 non-refundable booking deposit is required at the time of scheduling. This will not be refunded for any reason.

Damage Deposit:

A $400.00 damage, clean-up, and overtime deposit is required with the final payment. This is refundable 7 days after the event if there is no excessive cleaning, damage to the property, or overtime resulting from your event.

Event Liability Insurance:

We require an event liability insurance policy naming 'Rustic Meadows' as additional insured on the policy. The policy will be in the amount of not less than $1,000,000 per occurrence and must be provided to Rustic Meadows 30 days with final payment before the scheduled event. This insurance may be obtained through WedSafe.com or by an insurance agency of your choice.

Wine and Beer only at our venue:
